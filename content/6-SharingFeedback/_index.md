## Overall Evaluation

The working environment during my internship was professional, supportive, and conducive to learning. I was given the opportunity to work in a structured environment where processes, collaboration, and communication were clearly defined.

My mentor was highly supportive, always willing to guide, answer questions, and provide constructive feedback. This helped me quickly adapt to the workflow and gain confidence in handling assigned tasks.

Through this internship, I significantly broadened my understanding of cloud computing, real-world system architecture, and the practical application of technologies such as AWS services, backend systems, and full-stack workflows. I also gained valuable exposure to how real production systems are designed, operated, and improved over time.

## What I Learned

- Gained hands-on experience with cloud technologies and AWS services  
- Understood how a full-stack system operates from frontend to backend  
- Improved problem-solving and debugging skills in real-world scenarios  
- Learned how to work in a team environment and follow professional workflows  
- Enhanced my ability to read, understand, and analyze large codebases  

## Feedback And Suggestions

Although the internship experience was very valuable, I would like to suggest a few improvements:

- Providing more structured onboarding materials or documentation could help interns get up to speed faster  
- More real-world tasks or mini-projects could further enhance practical experience  
- Additional technical sharing sessions or workshops would be beneficial for deeper learning  

## Conclusion

Overall, this internship was a meaningful and rewarding experience. It not only strengthened my technical skills but also helped me develop a more professional mindset, better communication habits, and a clearer direction for my future career in software engineering and cloud computing.
