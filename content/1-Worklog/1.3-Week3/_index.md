### Week 3 Objectives

This week focused on developing the final project idea and practicing core AWS services such as EC2, S3, IAM, and RDS. The main objectives included:

- **Developing the final project**:
  - Building the project idea
  - Analyzing and selecting suitable AWS services
  - Drafting the initial architecture

- **Practicing EC2**:
  - Learning about the EC2 service
  - Deploying virtual machines
  - Configuring instances
  - Creating and using custom AMIs

- **Managing access and the development environment**:
  - Learning and practicing IAM Roles
  - Becoming familiar with Cloud9

- **Storage and data management**:
  - Configuring Amazon S3
  - Managing data and access permissions

- **Working with RDS databases**:
  - Creating a database
  - Managing and operating RDS

- **Reviewing and reinforcing knowledge**:
  - Reviewing IAM, EC2, S3, and RDS
  - Consolidating knowledge from Week 1 and Week 2
